What is readme?


Readme is a platform where anyone can read any book, at anytime.

Students nowadays have to borrow books from libraries and the book is issued only for a limited time, and we know how hard it is.

So, our team came up with a solution and decided to bring all the books you want on your nearest internet compatible devices, So now you don't have to worry about returning the books and finally you can say "Goodbyes" to the library.

Currently Readme is in a closed beta stage and we are planning to start a public beta very soon.

Have a great day :)


- Team Readme

Our Idea


We just thought about college students like us that how we manage to use books in our college. Each and every undergraduate course has number of semesters and in those semesters, we have to study for number of subjects. Not even a single subject repeats in another semester. Although, faculties provide multiple notes for their subjects, but still there are some suggested books by famous authors which are even also suggested by our teachers. But a student cannot buy books in every semester as those books left untouched just after the semester, even some of those books are not easily available and are also expensive.

So, our team worked on this thinking and created a portal just for college students where almost every book of every semester is uploaded there. The students can easily just search the book with their author’s name and they can read their books for free with us. Right now, we are at our very initial stage that we created this portal for our two streams: - BCA and BBA.

For these two streams almost, every book is available. If in case any author’s book is missing users can just email us; we will make sure that the required book is uploaded quickly.

Remember we are at our initial stage so the books are not available to download, they are just there to read online.
